Lighting
Lighting is a booklet designed to guide you through the exhibition of Bauhaus classics.
After extensive research and writing the texts, a timeless design was created, blending modern elements with Bauhaus aesthetics.

Drehbuch
Experience 14 different stories over 25 hours.
This publication contains 14 stories from class FG24, combined into a single screenplay.
The stories start at midnight and end at midnight, covering a full day on Earth and the lives of 14 fictional people.

Chat de Passage
I spent 12 days in the Passage du Grand Cerf in Paris, documenting this little world with sketches, photos, conversations, and texts that I wrote. From this, this book was created—an interactive children’s book inspired by a real story on site.
You and your child act as tourists there, and your exploration is constantly interrupted by a persistent ‘meow.’ Whether you choose to follow this cat or not is up to you.
You can repeatedly decide where your journey takes you next and through which door you want to go.
